Items Needed

  • Rice Grains
  • Rice Cooker
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Wash your rice with water in the rice cooker pot
  2. Pour out the water and put in more water (equivalent to the cups of rice put)
  3. Plug is and start the rice cooker with the rice cooker pot inside
  4. Wait until ready
