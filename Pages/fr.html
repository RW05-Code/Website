How to Make Fried Rice


    Rice Fried Rice Dehydrated Water

Fried

Items Needed

  • Cooking object (pan, wok, etc)
  • Cooked Rice (leftover)
  • Garlic
  • Oil
  • Egg(s)
  • Soy Sauce (or any other)

Instructions

  1. Cut garlic
  2. Add oil to cooking tool
  3. Crack in egg(s)
  4. Cook, but leave slightly raw
  5. Take out egg and add rice
  6. Cook rice for a bit, then add egg back in and split it apart
  7. Add seasoning
  8. Serve when done (Might require more steps for more ingredients)
